~ confucian ~ confucianism ~ confucianist ~ confucianists ~

There is no term in the Chinese language which directly corresponds to "confucianism".

The character rú (儒) meaning "scholar" is generally used both in past and present to refer to all things related to confucianism.

~ 儒 ~ rú ~ ruism ~ ruist ~ ruists ~

Other terms can be used depending on the situation, some of which are of modern origin:

~ 儒教 ~ rújiào ~ rujiao ~ 儒家 ~ rújiā ~ rujia ~ 儒学 ~ 儒學 ~ rúxué ~ ruxue ~

~ 孔教 ~ kǒngjiào ~ kongjiao ~ 孔家店 ~ kǒngjiādiàn ~ kongjiadian ~

~ 孔庙 ~ kǒngmiào ~ kongmiao ~ 君子 ~ jūnzǐ ~ junzi ~ 孔丘 ~ kǒngqiū ~ kongqui ~
